Welcome to Shibarium Classic Ecosytem

Shibarium classic A fully deflationary token that will appreciate with every single successive transaction. $SHIBAC’s deflationary mechanisms include a burn and redistribution system. 1% of each transaction is burned, and 2% is distributed as rewards to holders.

Tokenomics

Max. supply

1,000,000,000 $SHIBAC No mint function in the contract.

Buy/Sell fees 4%/3%

2% Holders Redistribution, 1% Burn, 1% Marketing.

Ownership

Contract Renounce : Owner can't change fees, Owner can't set max tx amount, Owner can't mint tokens, Owner can't blacklist wallet, Owner can't pause trading.

Deflationary

With 2% fees Redistribution to holders and 1% Burn, the token supply decreases with every sel and buy.
